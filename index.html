PART 4: SENTENCE STRUCTURE

PRACTICE TEST | 10-ITEM TEST | PASSING SCORE: 8

Directions: Choose the best sentence that follows correct grammar and proper sentence structure.

Answer Explanations:

  1. E – Dangling Modifier → Subject (he) matches the action.
  2. E – Sentence Fragment → Complete sentence. Others are fragments.
  3. C – Parallelism → All verbs are in the same form.
  4. E – Misplaced Modifier → Describes hikers clearly.
  5. C – Subject-Verb Agreement → "Bouquet" is singular → "was given."
  6. D – Run-on Sentence → Correctly joined with "and."
  7. E – Comma Splice → Fixed using "so."
  8. E – Apostrophe Misuse → Correct = teacher’s lounge.
  9. B – Word Order → Natural subject–verb–object order.
  10. E – Passive Voice → Active voice is stronger.
